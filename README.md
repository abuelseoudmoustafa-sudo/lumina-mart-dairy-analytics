# 🧀 Lumina Mart Dairy Division | End-to-End Data Analytics Project

## 📌 Overview

This project analyzes Lumina Mart’s dairy division using Power BI to uncover hidden risks behind strong revenue growth and provide actionable business recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Business Question

Is the current growth sustainable?

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

* Revenue: 108M EGP
* Profit: 26M EGP
* Growth: 50%
* Return Rate: 0.36%
* Revenue Health Score: 0.78

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* ⚠️ 53% of revenue comes from promotions
* 🧀 Cheese drives revenue but also returns
* 💸 42% of customers are price sensitive
* 📉 Profit margins under pressure

---

## 💼 Business Impact

* 💰 +5M EGP potential improvement
* 📉 Reduced return losses
* 📊 Improved margin stability
* ❤️ Better customer retention

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
