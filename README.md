# 🧀 Lumina Mart Dairy Division | End-to-End Data Analytics Project

## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes the dairy division of Lumina Mart using data analytics and Power BI to uncover hidden risks behind strong revenue growth and transform raw data into actionable business insights.

The goal is to move beyond surface-level metrics and evaluate the true quality and sustainability of business performance.

---

## 🎯 Business Question
**Is the current growth sustainable, or is it driven by hidden risks affecting profitability?**

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- 💰 Revenue: 108M EGP  
- 📈 Profit: 26M EGP  
- 🚀 Growth Rate: 50%  
- 🔁 Return Rate: 0.36%  
- 📊 Revenue Health Score: 0.78  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- ⚠️ 53% of revenue is driven by promotions  
- 🧀 Cheese is the top-selling product but also a major source of returns  
- 💸 42% of customers are price-sensitive  
- 📉 Profit margins are under pressure despite strong sales  

---

## 💼 Business Impact
- 💰 +5M EGP potential profit improvement  
- 📉 Reduced return-related losses  
- 📊 Improved margin stability  
- ❤️ Better customer retention strategies  

---

## 🔄 Project Workflow

### 1. Data Collection
Data was gathered from multiple business sources including:
- Sales transactions  
- Customer data  
- Product and logistics data  

---

### 2. Data Cleaning
- Handling missing values  
- Removing duplicates  
- Fixing inconsistencies  
- Standardizing formats  

---

### 3. Data Transformation
- Creating calculated fields (Profit, Margin, etc.)  
- Converting data types  
- Feature engineering  

---

### 4. Data Modeling
- Implemented **Star Schema**
  - Fact Table: Sales  
  - Dimension Tables: Products, Customers, Date  
- Built relationships between tables  

---

### 5. DAX Measures
Created key metrics using DAX:
- Revenue  
- Profit  
- Profit Margin  
- Growth Rate  
- Return Rate  

---

### 6. Dashboard Development
- Interactive Power BI dashboard  
- Filters by product, time, and customer  
- Visualizations for trends, KPIs, and comparisons  

---

## 📊 Key Findings Summary
Although Lumina Mart shows strong revenue growth, deeper analysis reveals:

- Growth is heavily dependent on promotions  
- Profitability is not increasing at the same rate  
- Certain high-performing products generate hidden losses  
- Customer loyalty is influenced primarily by pricing  

---

## 📌 Conclusion
This project demonstrates that high sales do not necessarily indicate strong business performance.

To achieve sustainable growth, Lumina Mart should:
- Reduce reliance on discounts  
- Focus on profitability rather than volume  
- Leverage data as a strategic decision-making tool
